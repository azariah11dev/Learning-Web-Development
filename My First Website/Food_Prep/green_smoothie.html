Green Broccoli Sprout Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup fresh broccoli sprouts
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1-2 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
  • 1/2 tsp spirulina powder (optional, for extra nutrients)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a thicker smoothie)

Instructions:

  1. Add the almond milk, broccoli sprouts, banana, pineapple chunks, mango chunks, chia seeds, honey or maple syrup (if using), and spirulina powder (if using) to a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are well combined and the smoothie is creamy. If you prefer a thicker consistency, add a few ice cubes and blend again.
  3. Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness if needed by adding more honey or maple syrup.
  4. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately!
