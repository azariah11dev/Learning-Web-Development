Welcome to Food Preparation Tips

Here are some useful tips for preparing food safely and efficiently:

  • Always wash your hands before handling food.
  • Use separate cutting boards for raw meat and vegetables.
  • Cook food to the appropriate temperature to kill harmful bacteria.
  • Store perishable items in the refrigerator promptly.
  • Keep your kitchen clean and sanitized.
